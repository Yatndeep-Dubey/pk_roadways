About Us

PK Roadways set sail on its journey in 1988. Since then, the service has become synonymous with reliability, responsiveness, and transparency in logistics solutions for parcel & part loads, truck load services, and textile consignments. We combine technology — real-time tracking, auto status alerts, and a smart mobile app — with hands-on operational excellence to deliver peace of mind to our clients.

Our Mission

To expand across states and provide the best-managed, most-profitable logistics services using technology and people.
